This Privacy Policy explains how Zap Mudra ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, shares and protects your personal information when you use our website and services. By using our website, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

  • Details you give us: name, mobile number, email, city, the product you're interested in, income details, and any information you enter in our forms.
  • KYC / financial information: where required to process a loan or credit-card application (e.g. PAN, Aadhaar, income proof).
  • Automatically collected data: device, browser, IP address and usage information collected through cookies and similar technologies.

2. How we use your information

  • To assess your requirement and match you with suitable loan or credit-card offers.
  • To share your application details with relevant banks / NBFCs so they can process it.
  • To contact you about your enquiry, application status and related offers.
  • To improve our website, services and customer experience.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Sharing your information

We share your information with our partner banks and NBFCs strictly to process the products you apply for, and with service providers who help us operate (such as hosting or communication tools). We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information where required by law.

4. Consent to be contacted

By submitting your details, you authorise Zap Mudra and its partners to contact you via call, SMS, WhatsApp or email regarding your enquiry, even if you are registered on the DND / NDNC list.

5. Data security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to remember preferences and understand how the site is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may withdraw consent, by contacting us using the details below. Some data may be retained where required by law or by our partners.
